FAMOUS SINGERS

Devji Thakor

Devji Thakor — a powerhouse of rhythm, melody, and timeless devotion to music. With a journey spanning over 25+ years, Devji is a true master of both Garba and Bollywood music. For the past 2 years he has enchanted international audiences in Hong Kong, while remaining an integral part of the renowned Tahukar Beats back in Gujarat.

Instagram: @iamdevjithakor
YouTube: Devji Thakor Official

Hetal Sadhu

Hetal Sadhu — a renowned Gujarati folk singer from Ahmedabad. Captivating audiences for over 10+ years, she is a key member of the Tahuko musical group. Her soulful voice and energetic performances have earned her immense love across Gujarat and beyond.

Instagram: hetal_sadhu_official
YouTube: @HetalSadhuOfficial

Nikky Mevada

Nikky Mevada — grace, devotion, and musical brilliance in one voice. A shining star for over 15+ years, born 25-03-1998. She mesmerizes audiences with soulful Gujarati Garba and performs with Nikky Beats, Visnagar, organized by Bhavin Mevada.

Instagram: the_nikky_mevada_official
Instagram: the_nikky_mevada_live
YouTube: Nikky Mevada Official

Sonu Charan

Sonu Charan (real name Sonal Gadhvi) — a celebrated Gujarati folk singer from Ahmedabad. Known for powerful Garba performances, she has been in the singing profession for over 15 years and commands over 2,03,000+ followers on Instagram.

Instagram: thesonucharan
YouTube: @sonucharan05
Facebook: Sonu Charan

Smita Makwana

Smita Makwana — a multifaceted personality from Gujarat renowned for her significant contributions to the state's cultural landscape. A talented singer who has also ventured into acting, she embodies the vibrant cultural spirit of Gujarat through her versatile artistry and strong social media presence.

Instagram: smitamakwanaofficial

Kishan Raval

Kishan Raval — a renowned Gujarati Folk Singer from the culturally rich village of Laghnaj. Captivating audiences for over 15+ years, his soulful voice and deep connection with the audience have earned him immense love across Gujarat and beyond.
